The #1 College Search App for iOS and Android

Challenge

Most of the information needed to decide on the right college is scattered across the web, with many sources containing outdated, salesman-like language. College counselors are often busy, making it difficult for students to get 1 on 1 time.

Solution

Capture accurate and current college data from trusted sources, and present the most relevant information in an unbiased manner for students to make the right decision. Provide 1 on 1 chat with seasoned college counselors to answer any and all questions about the college process.

ROI

With data science and a sleek UI, the Schoold App became the #1 college search app in the App Store and Google Play Store.
